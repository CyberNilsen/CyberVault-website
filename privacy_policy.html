Privacy Policy

Welcome to CyberVault's Privacy Policy. Your privacy is our top priority, and we want to be clear about how we handle your data.

Local Storage and Security: CyberVault is designed to keep your data completely private. All your passwords, account information, personal notes, and other sensitive data are stored locally on your device and protected using AES-256 encryption. We never store your data on remote servers or in the cloud.

No Data Collection: We believe in absolute privacy. CyberVault:

  • Does not collect any personal information
  • Does not track your usage or behavior
  • Does not share any data with third parties
  • Does not store information on remote servers
  • Operates entirely offline for your security

Your Data Control: You have complete control over your information. You can export, delete, or transfer your data between devices at any time. No backups are created without your explicit action, and your master password never leaves your device. All encryption and decryption happen locally on your device, ensuring your sensitive information remains private.

Security Measures: Your data is protected using industry-standard encryption methods. No plain text data is ever stored, and all sensitive information is encrypted using AES-256 encryption. The application operates offline by design, eliminating the risk of unauthorized access through network connections.

Policy Updates: This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the application, and you will be notified when opening the app after an update.

By using CyberVault, you can be confident that your data remains private, secure, and under your control at all times.
