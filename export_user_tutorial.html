Export/Import User Tutorial

Learn how to safely transfer your CyberVault user data between devices

1
Export Your User Data

Export your user data to a location where you can easily find the files. In this tutorial, we'll use the desktop for simplicity.

IMPORTANT!ã…¤ NEVER SHARE THESE FILES WITH ANYONE EXCEPT YOURSELF!
2
Transfer Files to New PC

Transfer the exported files to your other computer using your preferred method (USB drive, cloud storage, etc.).

3
Open CyberVault on New PC

Go to your other PC and open the CyberVault application.

4
Import User Data

On the login screen, click "Import a user" and select the exported files from your desktop.

5
Login to Your Account

Login to your user account using your username and password. Your data will now be available on your new PC!
